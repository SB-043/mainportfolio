CyberSecurity: Phishing Attacks

A compact guide to the most common and dangerous forms of phishing‚Äîdesigned to raise awareness and strengthen digital defenses. This sheet breaks down:

  • Email Phishing: fake messages with malicious links or attachments
  • Spear Phishing: personalized attacks targeting individuals
  • Whaling: high-stakes scams aimed at executives and officials
  • Smishing: deceptive SMS messages from fake senders
  • Vishing: voice calls impersonating trusted organizations
  • Pharming: redirecting users to fake websites via DNS poisoning
  • Clone Phishing: replicas of real emails with hidden threats
  • BEC (Business Email Compromise): fraud targeting companies and vendors
Each section includes examples, warning signs, and practical tips for staying safe. Ideal for students, professionals, and anyone navigating the digital world.
